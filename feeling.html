feeling

A photography project i completed in may 2020. Photos were taken with a dslr camera and edited with photoshop.

My goal was to capture the intricate beauty of various textures; and to relate those feelings in the physical sense to feelings (thoughts, memories, and emotions) in the abstract sense.
