grids (2023-24)

I spent most of 2024 putting together these grids. This project is an experiment in communication, using what i've learned from my research into art history and theory. In each grid, i've arranged recreations of existing artworks into configurations that in/evoke broader ideas. The panels' configuration suggests their relationship; the form, content, and context of each panel allows it to speak for itself.
