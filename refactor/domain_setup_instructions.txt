Hey Pavel! Here's how to connect your new domain:

Step 1: Cloudflare DNS settings

After you buy the domain, go to the DNS settings page in Cloudflare and add these 5 records:

Type: A      | Name: @   | Content: 185.199.108.153
Type: A      | Name: @   | Content: 185.199.109.153
Type: A      | Name: @   | Content: 185.199.110.153
Type: A      | Name: @   | Content: 185.199.111.153
Type: CNAME  | Name: www | Content: pivarshev.github.io

If Cloudflare asks about proxy status, set them all to "DNS only" (gray cloud, not orange).

Step 2: GitHub repo settings

Go to https://github.com/pivarshev/pivarshev.github.io/settings/pages

Under "Custom domain", type your new domain (e.g. pivarshev.com) and click Save.

Step 3: Enable HTTPS

Wait a few minutes, then refresh that same Settings > Pages page. Check the box that says "Enforce HTTPS".

Step 4: Wait

Give it 5-10 minutes for everything to connect. Then visit your new domain and it should show your site.
