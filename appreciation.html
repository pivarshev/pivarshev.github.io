appreciation
(2021-22)

Before my senior year of college, i created a fully original and inspired painting of my beautiful friend Mihir. Over the next few months, i made more artworks celebrating my dearest friends. I exhibited them at the end of the year. I didn't like them at the time because they felt too emotive, even too sincere. But now i look back at them with love, because that's ultimately what they were always meant to mean.
